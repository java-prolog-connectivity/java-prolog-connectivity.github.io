    Try it !

    JPC sources are available at GitHub.

    In case you are using Maven, it is also available at the Sonatype Spanshots repository. Just add this dependency to your POM to include JGum into your project. 

    <dependency>
  <groupId>com.github.java-prolog-connectivity</groupId>
  <artifactId>jpc</artifactId>
  <version>0.0.1-alpha-SNAPSHOT</version>
</dependency>

    You may also find interesting taking a look at the JPC API documentation.
